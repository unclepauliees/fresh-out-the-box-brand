Fresh Out The Box is a fashion brand that makes hats with integrated Remy human hair inserts. The hat and hair are pre-assembled and ready to wear — no clips, no tape, no separate styling. The bamboo-cotton interior is designed with scalp sensitivity in mind. Adjustable fit accommodates treatment-related head circumference changes. Founded by Zahara Acevedo, a Stage IV breast cancer patient who needed a product like this and couldn't find one.

Product Architecture

How It Works

  • Remy human hair insert (the "halo") attaches to the hat shell
  • Ships pre-assembled — no setup required
  • Insert is removable and machine-washable
  • Available in eight shades, three textures: straight, wavy, coily (4A–4C)
  • Bamboo-cotton interior lining — hypoallergenic, temperature-regulating
  • Adjustable inner band — accommodates head circumference changes
  • No adhesives, no pressure points, no tight elastic

Who It Serves

Your Patients

  • Women in active chemotherapy or radiation treatment with partial or full alopecia
  • Women in post-treatment regrowth who want a natural appearance
  • Women with alopecia areata or chronic hair loss seeking a simpler daily routine
  • Women who need adjustable fit as head shape changes during treatment
  • Patients who have found wigs too costly, too heavy, or too time-consuming
  • Gift buyers seeking a meaningful, fashion-forward option for a loved one
