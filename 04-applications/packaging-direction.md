# Fresh Out The Box — Packaging Direction

The name is the packaging brief. "Fresh out the box" is what happens the moment she opens it. Every physical detail must be designed for that single moment — not as logistics, but as the product experience's opening act.

---

## The Brand Promise, Made Physical

The box is not a shipping container. It is the moment the brand name enacts itself.

When she opens the box:
- She sees the inside lid copy first — a short line that speaks to her, not at her
- She lifts tissue paper sealed with the brand mark
- She finds a hat that is already ready to wear — pre-assembled, pre-styled
- She sees the insert card before the care instructions
- The unboxing takes thirty seconds and feels like a gift she gave herself

No "we care about your journey." No pink bows. No hospital-pamphlet insert. A well-made hat in a well-made box, with copy that treats her like the stylish person she is.

## Box Specifications

### Primary Carton (Hero Kit)
- **Form:** Rigid setup box, lift-off lid
- **Dimensions (interior):** Approximately 14" × 10" × 5" — accommodates one hat with insert attached, plus insert card
- **Construction:** Heavy-duty chipboard (minimum 2mm), cloth-wrapped or premium matte laminate
- **Exterior finish:** Matte laminate, Espresso (#1E1510) base

### Exterior Lid (Top Face)
- **Color:** Espresso (#1E1510) full bleed
- **Logotype:** FOTB primary logotype centered, in Crème (#F5EDE3) — approximately 50% of lid width
- **No other graphic elements on the lid exterior.** Fashion-house restraint. White space communicates confidence.
- **Edge treatment:** No pattern or print on lid sides — continuation of Espresso ground

### Exterior Box Bottom (Sides)
- On the long side: FRESH OUT THE BOX in DM Sans Light, letter-spaced, Crème on Espresso
- On the short side: freshoutthebox.com
- **No barcodes on visible exterior faces.** Barcode and SKU label on the bottom face only.

---

## Interior Packaging

### Inside Lid (Interior Face)
This is the first thing visible when the lid is lifted. It should land as a quiet, warm surprise.

**Design:** Full-bleed Crème (#F5EDE3) interior lid
**Typography:** Playfair Display, 400 weight, 20–22pt
**Copy:**

```
The hat. The hair.
You, out the door.
```

Centered, generous vertical spacing. No decorative elements — the copy is the design.

---

### Tissue Paper
- **Color:** Warm Sand (#C9B49A) tissue, or Crème tissue with a very subtle tone-on-tone repeat of the FOTB halo mark
- **Volume:** One full layer covering the product
- **Seal:** A small branded sticker — the halo mark only (no wordmark), Espresso on Crème circle, 1.5" diameter

---

### Product Placement
- Hat sits centered in the box, brim down (crown up) — the hair halo is the first thing visible when the tissue is lifted
- If the kit includes two inserts, the second insert is tucked underneath the hat, wrapped in a secondary tissue
- Insert card sits on top of the hat, slightly offset to the right — discoverable, not buried

---

### Insert Card

**Physical spec:** 4" × 6" card, Crème stock (minimum 120lb/325gsm), two-sided
- **Front:** Centered editorial quote in Playfair Display italic — "WELCOME TO YOURS." — with the brand mark below it
- **Back:** Care instructions, adjustable fit guide, reimbursement language (hedged), and contact information in DM Sans

## Secondary Packaging (Single Item, Non-Kit)

For individual hat or individual insert orders:
- Same rigid setup box construction, smaller form factor (~12" × 10" × 4")
- Same exterior treatment
- Inside lid copy adapts: *"Look like you."*
- Single tissue layer, branded seal
- Same insert card

---

## Gift Box (Gift Buyer SKU)

For the two-piece gift box:
- Same rigid setup box with interior divider — hat on left, insert in Crème pouch on right
- No gift message slot (brand should not mark this as a medical gift)
- No "For someone special who deserves to feel beautiful" copy — the box is the message
- Optional personalization: a blank quality card slot on the interior lid where a handwritten note can be placed — but the default includes no card

---

## Shipping Carton (Outer)

- Plain corrugated brown exterior, no exterior branding (privacy consideration — the recipient's health context is private)
- Packing slip inside: FOTB letterhead, order details only, no health-related copy
- Consider a discreet exterior label: "Gift Item — Handle with Care" as the only text, no brand name on the shipping exterior if customer has selected the "discreet shipping" option

**Discreet shipping should be the default, with opt-in visible exterior branding as the alternative.** The primary audience is often receiving packages at workplaces or in shared households.

---

## Materials Philosophy

| Component | Material Direction |
|---|---|
| Box board | Minimum 30% post-consumer recycled content |
| Tissue | FSC-certified paper, water-based dye |
| Sticker | Kraft or Crème-tone recycled stock |
| Insert card | 100% recycled content, minimum 30% post-consumer |
| Outer shipping | 100% recycled corrugated |

No single-use plastic anywhere in the unboxing. The bamboo/cotton lining of the product is the brand's material story — the packaging should extend that ethic visibly.

---

## What the Box Is Not

- Not pink. Not ribbon-adjacent in any visual element.
- Not clinical — no watermarks suggesting medical supply, no cross symbols, no red/white color adjacency
- Not overly precious — this is not a luxury beauty unboxing. It is a fashion brand with emotional intelligence. The difference: beauty unboxing is maximalist and performative; this is restrained and genuine.
- Not Instagrambait-first — the unboxing should feel good to the person doing it, not engineered for video content first. If it photographs well, that's a consequence of it being well-made.
