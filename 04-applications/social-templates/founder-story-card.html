Zahara Acevedo — Founder

She didn't find what she needed. So she built it.

During her own chemotherapy, Zahara saw a gap — between the headwear that looked clinical and the wigs that cost more than most people could absorb mid-treatment. Fresh Out The Box is what she made to close it.
